## What This Shows

Two minds collaborate. You hold the problem, context, intent, and judgment. AI holds all code knowledge, patterns, syntax, and libraries. The prompt bridges them. Software emerges.

## Key Insight

You never need to learn the right side. AI has it. Your job is only the left side: know your problem, provide context, state intent, apply judgment.
